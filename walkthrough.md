# App Store Screenshot Concepts

I have generated two distinct background container styles and applied them to your existing practice screenshots in `QuickDown/screenshots`. The compositions resize the backgrounds to the official App Store requirements (2560x1600, 16:10 aspect ratio), add rounded corners, and apply a multi-layered drop shadow to make the application window pop. 

The finalized output images are saved in `QuickDown/app-store-assets/` and the reusable Python compositing script has been placed at [QuickDown/scripts/compose_screenshots.py](file:///Users/Tennyson/QuickDown/scripts/compose_screenshots.py).
